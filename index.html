Cool bookmarklet, bro. But what does it do?

FitWeird is about as dumb as it gets. No screen based rulers, no visiting some site and typing in a URL. Click the bookmarklet and a little doodad will show up at the bottom of your browser. Resize your window, it tells you dimensions.

Next time someone says your responsive design "looks weird on an iPad", you can manually resize the window to the appropiate dimensions. Bingo bango.
