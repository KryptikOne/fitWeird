Cool bookmarklet, bro, but what does it do?

FitWeird is about as dumb as it gets. Resize your window, it tells you dimensions. This is great for adding/debugging breakpoints when things get weird.

No on-screen rulers, no typing a URL. Click the bookmarklet and a little doodad will show up at the top of your browser. Bingo bango.
